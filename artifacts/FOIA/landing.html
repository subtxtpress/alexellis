FAQ

Common questions

What types of agencies can I request from?
Federal agencies (covered by FOIA), and state and local agencies covered by state open records laws. Our directory includes 18,000+ agencies across all 50 states as well as state universities.
What happens if my request is denied?
FOIA.io can help you draft a legally-grounded administrativeappeal letter tailored to the specific reason for denial.
Is my account data private?
Yes. Your requests, letters, and documents are visible only to you and are not shared with any third party.
How is the free plan limited?
The free plan allows up to 5 active requests at a time. All core features—letter drafting, the agency directory, deadline tracking, and notes—are included. Upgrading to Pro removes the request limit and adds PDF export and priority request & appeal letter generation.
